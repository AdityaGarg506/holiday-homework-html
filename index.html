Animation


Animation in Krita is a fun and creative way to make drawings come to life. Krita is a free drawing program that lets you create frame-by-frame animations, just like a flipbook. You can draw each frame on a timeline and then play them to see your character move or tell a short story. It has helpful tools like onion skinning, which lets you see the previous and next drawings to make smoother movements. Krita is a great way for beginners and students to explore animation and learn how cartoons are made!
